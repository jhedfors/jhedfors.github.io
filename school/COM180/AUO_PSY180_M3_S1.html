Module 3 Overview

As you learned in the previous modules, many factors influence the way that we think and communicate as individuals. While understanding the self is an important part, communication involves other people—which means that we must learn to understand others in order to be interpersonally effective.

What motivates us to do the things that we do? What motivates others to do the things that they do? In this module, we will examine motivation and how it affects our lives and our interpersonal effectiveness. We will also examine the impact of culture on who we are, how we understand ourselves, and how we interpret others.
