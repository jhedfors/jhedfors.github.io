Identity Development

Identity is an individual's self-definition that encompasses the enduring characteristics of the self. Understanding the self from various perspectives is important because who you are and what you think of yourself influence all of the different facets of your life.

People view themselves through two perspectives—self-concept and self-esteem. Our self-concept is what we think about ourselves whereas our self-esteem is what we feel about ourselves (O’Connell, O’Connell, & Kuntz, 2005). Self-concept involves the beliefs you hold about who you are and the characteristics you have, while self-esteem is your overall positive or negative evaluation of yourself (Duffy, Shaw, Scott, & Tepper, 2005).
